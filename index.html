WEB DEVELOPMENT

Basic Introduction

Web Development is the work involved in developing a website for the internet. Web development can range from developing a simple static page of plain text to complex web based internet applications(web apps).

As an Industry

The growth of this industry is being driven by buissness wishing to use their website to advertise and sell products and services to the customers.
